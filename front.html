HISTORY OF JUSTJAM

Becoming India’s no. 1 fashion destination is not an easy feat. Sincere efforts, digital enhancements and a team of dedicated personnel with an equally loyal customer base have made JustJam the online platform that it is today. The original B2B venture for personalized gifts was conceived in 2007 but transitioned into a full-fledged ecommerce giant within a span of just a few years. By 2012, JustJam had introduced 350 Indian and international brands to its platform, and this has only grown in number each passing year. Today JustJam sits on top of the online fashion game with an astounding social media following, a loyalty program dedicated to its customers, and tempting, hard-to-say-no-to deals.

The JustJam shopping website came into existence in the year 2015 to further encourage customers’ shopping sprees. Visit the website on your Android or IOS device this very minute to experience fashion like never before
